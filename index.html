Work Faster, Not Harder

Tired of typing? Dictate notes, emails, and messages in any app. Wyspe integrates seamlessly into your workflow to save you time and effort.

Keep Your Conversations Private

Your voice is your own. All transcription is processed securely on your device. Your data is never sent to the cloud, ensuring complete privacy.

Stay in Your Flow

With a simple global hotkey (⌘+⌘), Wyspe is always ready when you need it and invisible when you don't. No need to switch windows or lose focus.

Simple to Use

2

Start talking. Your words will appear instantly in the active application.

3

Privacy is Not an Afterthought. It's the Default.

Wyspe uses on-device machine learning to transcribe your speech. This means your voice recordings and transcriptions are never uploaded to any server, ensuring your sensitive information stays with you.
